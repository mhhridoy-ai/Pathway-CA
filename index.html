Our story

Built from the inside

"I learned everything the hard way. PathwayCA exists so you don't have to."

Our founder moved from Bangladesh to Canada as an international student — just like you. They experienced every challenge firsthand: the confusion of choosing the right program, the stress of finding housing, the loneliness of settling into a new culture, and the difficulty of landing a first job in Canada.

Through years of trial, error, and adaptation, they built a deep understanding of what it really takes to succeed as a Bangladeshi student in Canada — not just academically, but personally and professionally.

PathwayCA was built to share that hard-won knowledge with every student who follows the same path. No sugarcoating. No empty promises. Just practical, honest, real-world guidance.
